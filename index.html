Our products

Grocery shops

Providing capital for inventory management, expansion of store space, and modernization of operations. Support is also provided to local grocery businesses in managing their cash flow.

Medicines shops

We facilitate the purchase of essential medicines, upgrading storage facilities to maintain proper temperature and hygiene, and implementing inventory management systems to optimize the stock levels.

Electronic shops

Supporting acquisition of new goods, marketing campaigns to reach a wider customer base, financing purchase of high-demand items, funding renovation and improving the store's physical space.

Mobile stores

Helping retailers secure the inventory, upgrade their display units, and expand into new products. Customized loans are provided to enhance product offerings and meet customer demands.

Pet stores

We support the acquisition of a diverse inventory of pet supplies, expansion of product lines (e.g., pet grooming services), and marketing efforts to attract new customers.

Our Service

Digital Process

Eliminating the need for extensive paperwork and in-person visits, making the application process convenient and accessible. Applicants can complete the process through our user-friendly mobile app.

Quick Approval

Retail PE leverages data analytics to provide loan approvals within a significantly shorter timeframe compared to traditional banks. This speed allows businesses to access capital quickly.

Digital Disbursement

Funds are directly transferred to the borrower's account, ensuring transparency and speed. This eliminates delays & complexities associated with traditional cheque-based disbursement methods.

Easy EMI Options

We offer flexible repayment schedules that are tailored to the individual business's cash flow and this assures that the payments are aligned with the revenue generation cycles of the business.
